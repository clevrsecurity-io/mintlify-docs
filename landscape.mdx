## The one slide

<Info>
<span>Say this.</span> "Visibility tells you what happened. Identity tells you who. Gateways route the call. Clevr is the only layer that <b>decides the action before it runs</b>, deterministically, and hands you a <b>signed receipt</b>. In the EU, on infrastructure you control."
</Info>

## Where we concede

<ul>
<li>Credential vaulting / OAuth-on-behalf-of at scale → identity/NHI vendors lead; we mint scoped creds on-permit, we don't hoard.</li>
<li>Thousands of pre-built tools / breadth → gateways/marketplaces lead; we govern the ecosystem, we don't rebuild it.</li>
<li>Full-suite platform coverage → the giants; we're the sharp, provable, sovereign wedge.</li>
</ul>
