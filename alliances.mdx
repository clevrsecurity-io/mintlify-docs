<p className="lede">Identity and gateway vendors own the plane above us and need the decision we are. Co-sell downstream of identity, and arm every partner with the same repeatable kit.</p>

## Alliance targets & the narrative

<ul>
<li><b>IdP (Okta, Entra, Ping)</b>, "IdP authority ends at issuance." They authenticate the agent; we decide the action and sign it. Joint story to the same CISO.</li>
<li><b>MCP gateways / agent runtimes</b>, their governance hook is an empty slot; Clevr fills it (a signed decision + behavioral cascade). Integrate rather than collide.</li>
<li><b>Cloud / sovereign hosts</b>, EU sovereign-cloud partners for regulated hosting and marketplace listing.</li>
<li><b>GRC / ITSM (ServiceNow, Jira)</b>, export signed receipts into the audit system of record.</li>
</ul>

## Rules of engagement

<ul>
<li>Partner sources → partner leads, Clevr supports. Clevr sources → we may bring a partner for delivery/sovereignty.</li>
<li>One deal desk owns registration + conflict; decisions in days, not weeks.</li>
<li>Never name a competitor's client or Clevr in a rival's system; keep it clean.</li>
</ul>
