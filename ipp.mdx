title: "Partner profile & economics"

<p className="lede">Recruit few, enable deeply. The right partner already sells security or AI programs into our ICP and wants a sticky, recurring, sovereignty-aligned line.</p>

## Ideal partner profile

<table>
<thead><tr><th>Signal</th><th>Green flag</th><th>Red flag</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td>Book of business</td><td>CISOs / regulated EU accounts already</td><td>SMB volume, no security buyer</td></tr>
<tr><td>Capability</td><td>Delivers IAM, SOC/MDR, or AI/agent builds</td><td>Pure logo-resale, no delivery</td></tr>
<tr><td>Motion</td><td>Wants recurring managed/services margin</td><td>One-off transactional only</td></tr>
<tr><td>Sovereignty</td><td>EU-based, sells "data stays in-country"</td><td>US-only, no residency story</td></tr>
</tbody>
</table>

## Tiers & economics

<table>
<thead><tr><th>Tier</th><th>Commitment</th><th>Reward (indicative)</th><th>Support</th></tr></thead>
<tbody>
<tr><td><b>Registered</b></td><td>Referrals, trained on the pitch</td><td>Referral fee on closed-won</td><td>Deal reg + assets</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Select (Resell/SI)</b></td><td>Owns paper or delivery; annual target</td><td>Reseller margin + services pull-through</td><td>Named SE support, MDF on approval</td></tr>
<tr><td><b>Managed (MSSP)</b></td><td>Multi-tenant, operates governance</td><td>Wholesale platform rate; partner sets retail</td><td>Delegated admin, partner RBAC, roadmap access</td></tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<Warning>
<span>Numbers are indicative.</span> Exact margins/fees are set per agreement and are not published. Keep economics in the deal desk, not in a deck.
</Warning>

## Deal registration

<ul>
<li>Register the account + use case; first-to-register with an active opportunity gets protection for a fixed window.</li>
<li>Protection = margin/referral integrity + no direct competition on that named opportunity.</li>
<li>Channel conflict resolved by the deal desk; default to protecting the partner who sourced.</li>
</ul>
