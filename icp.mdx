<p className="lede">We win where agents take real actions in regulated or high-consequence environments, and where someone already answers to an auditor.</p>

<table><tbody><tr><td>Size</td><td>500+ employees (enterprise); design partners can be smaller if agent-forward.</td></tr><tr><td>Geography</td><td>EU-first (sovereignty wedge), then UK / regulated global.</td></tr><tr><td>Industries</td><td>BFSI, healthcare, public sector, telco, critical infrastructure, MSSPs.</td></tr><tr><td>Trigger</td><td>Agents / copilots moving from pilot to production with real tool access.</td></tr><tr><td>Governance stack</td><td>Has an IdP (Okta/Entra), ITSM (ServiceNow/Jira), audit pressure (DORA/NIS2/AI Act/SOC2).</td></tr></tbody></table>

## Qualify the fit

<table>
<thead><tr><th>Must-have</th><th>Nice-to-have</th><th>Red flag</th></tr></thead>

</tbody>
</table>
