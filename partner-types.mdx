## What makes Clevr channel-ready

<ul>
<li><b>Multi-tenant + delegated admin</b> and per-tenant scoping, one partner, many clients.</li>
<li><b>Per-tenant signed audit</b>, the partner's clients each get their own tamper-evident evidence.</li>
<li><b>Single-tenant VM option</b>, sovereignty and data-residency stories partners can sell.</li>
<li><b>Observe mode</b>, a zero-risk land the partner can run in a first engagement.</li>
</ul>
