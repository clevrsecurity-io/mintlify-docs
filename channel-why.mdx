<p className="lede">Regulated-EU buyers trust local, accountable delivery. The channel is how we reach them at scale, MSSPs run governance as a service, SIs embed us in agent programs, resellers carry the paper. Direct proves the wedge; channel multiplies it.</p>

## Why channel, why now

<ul>
<li><b>Reach & trust.</b> EU CISOs buy through partners they already trust for sovereignty and support.</li>
<li><b>Delivery leverage.</b> Governance is operated, not just bought, MSSPs and SIs supply the hands.</li>
<li><b>Distribution.</b> Marketplaces and IdP/gateway alliances put us in an existing buying motion.</li>
</ul>

<Warning>
<span>Sequencing.</span> Land <b>5-6 direct reference wins first</b>. Channel amplifies a proven motion; it doesn't create one. Recruit partners against a repeatable pitch + a signed-receipt proof, not a whiteboard.
</Warning>

## Three channel bets (in order)

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Bet 1 · MSSP, governance as a service">
  Managed agent governance for regulated mid-market who won't staff it. Recurring, sticky, sovereignty-aligned.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bet 2 · SI / consultancy, embed in agent programs">
  Whoever builds the client's agents makes Clevr the guardrail. Services pull-through.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bet 3 · Tech alliances, co-sell downstream of identity">
  IdP and gateway vendors need the decision layer we are. Co-sell, don't compete.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
