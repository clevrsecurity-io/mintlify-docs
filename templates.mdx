<p className="lede">Short, specific, outcome-first. Name the action gap; offer a zero-risk observe pilot.</p>

## Outbound opener, CISO

```text
Subject: who decides what your agents are allowed to do?

Hi {First},

Your teams are shipping agents that take real actions, tools, data, maybe payments.
Your IdP says who each agent is. What decides whether a given action should run,
before it runs, and proves it afterward?

Clevr sits in-path and returns allow / hold / block on every consequential action,
deterministically, with a signed receipt your auditor can verify.

Worth a 20-min fit check? We can start in observe mode, zero production risk.
```

## Champion follow-up

```text
We'll wire one agent + one path (MCP or your SDK) in observe mode and show
would-allow / would-deny with reasons. If the evidence looks right, we flip on
scoped enforcement on a single route. No agent-code change, no rip-and-replace.
```

## Close email

```text
Attached: the pilot report, actions allowed vs held vs blocked, and a signed
receipt exported to {ServiceNow/Jira}. Proposed next step: expand to {second path}
and add the Compliance & Evidence pack for your {DORA/NIS2/AI Act} mapping.
```

## Partner recruit, MSSP

```text
Your clients are turning on agents and can't staff governance. Add Managed Agent
Governance to your catalogue: one multi-tenant console to decide and sign every
agent action, per client, in-region. Recurring margin, a sovereignty story, and
signed evidence that shortens their audits. 30 min to see the multi-tenant console?
```
