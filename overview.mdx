<p className="lede">Clevr is the runtime governance layer for AI agents. It sits in the path of every consequential action an agent takes and decides <b>allow</b>, <b>hold</b> (human approval), or <b>block</b>, before the action runs, then signs the decision as tamper-evident evidence.</p>

<Info>
<span>Anchor line.</span> "We don't just see what agents do. We decide whether each action is allowed, before it runs, and we prove it."
</Info>

## What this playbook is

<p>A single source of truth for how we position, qualify, sell, and channel Clevr. It replaces the Nov-2025 v2 (which framed us as SOAR/auto-remediation "machine actions"). We now lead with <b>AI agents</b>, the <b>action gap</b>, and a <b>signed decision</b>.</p>
<ul>
<li><b>Direct motion</b>, ICP, personas, messaging, discovery, demo, pilot, objections, competitive.</li>
<li><b>Channel motion</b>, MSSP, resellers, SIs, tech alliances: how we reach regulated EU accounts at scale.</li>
<li><b>Assets</b>, pricing, email/call templates, one-page talk track.</li>
</ul>
