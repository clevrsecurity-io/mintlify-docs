## The MSSP pitch (partner-facing)

<Info>
"Add <b>Managed Agent Governance</b> to your catalogue: decide and sign every agent action for your clients, from one multi-tenant console. New recurring margin, a sovereignty story that wins EU deals, and per-client signed evidence that shortens their audits."
</Info>

## Why it fits an MSSP

<ul>
<li><b>Recurring + sticky</b>, governance is operated monthly, not a one-time install.</li>
<li><b>Multi-tenant</b>, one console, delegated admin, partner RBAC, per-tenant policy + audit.</li>
<li><b>Differentiated SOC service</b>, from "we watch your agents" to "we decide and prove their actions".</li>
<li><b>Sovereignty</b>, EU-hostable; the MSSP keeps client data in-region.</li>
</ul>

## What the client gets

<ul>
<li>Agent actions decided in-path (allow/hold/block) under policies the MSSP tunes.</li>
<li>Their own tamper-evident, <b>signed</b> ledger, audit-ready, exportable to their GRC.</li>
<li>No new hire; the MSSP runs it, escalates only what needs a human.</li>
</ul>

## Enablement checklist

<ul>
<li>Multi-tenant onboarding + delegated-admin runbook.</li>
<li>Starter policy packs per vertical (BFSI, healthcare, public sector).</li>
<li>Client-facing observe-mode report template (the land).</li>
<li>Escalation / approval workflow into the MSSP's SOC tooling.</li>
</ul>
